Are the savings worth it?

Cost saved is what you spent vs. always picking the most expensive model. Quality kept is the average quality of the model that was actually picked, divided by the average quality of the best-known model for each request type. If quality kept stays high while cost saved is high, you're winning. If quality drops fast, you're saving money but making users mad.
