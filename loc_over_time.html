Methodology: for each month, the last commit on or before the 1st of the next month was selected. Lines were counted with git grep -c "" <commit> -- '*.py' '*.ts' '*.tsx' '*.js' '*.jsx', excluding paths under node_modules, .next, _experimental/out, dist, and build. Module buckets: litellm/proxy/, litellm/llms/, litellm/integrations/, litellm/types/, litellm/core (everything else under litellm/: main.py, router*, utils.py, caching/, etc.), ui/ (Next.js dashboard), tests/, enterprise/, litellm-proxy-extras/, and other (cookbook/, scripts/, CI). The drop in litellm/core in Sep 2024 is a code reorg — about 62k lines moved into tests/ that month (and the ui/ bucket also appeared in volume that month).
